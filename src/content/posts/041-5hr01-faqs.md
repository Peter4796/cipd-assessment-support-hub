Here are answers to the questions learners ask most about 5HR01, Employment relationship management. Always check the specifics against your own current brief and centre guidance.

### How much employment law do I need to know?

Enough to handle the essentials accurately and apply them, not the depth of a legal specialist. Focus on contracts, dismissal, discrimination and fair procedure, and always apply the law to scenarios.

### Can I use my own organisation?

Yes, and it usually makes your analysis stronger. Use real examples of voice, conflict handling or procedures from your workplace where you can.

### Does UK law apply if I study in the UAE?

It depends on your brief and centre. Many briefs use UK-style principles, but be clear which legal context you are writing in and check what your centre expects.

### Why do people get referred on 5HR01?

Most often for stating the law without applying it, using out-of-date legal points, or describing rather than analysing. Our common mistakes guide covers how to avoid these.

> The theme across all of these: apply the law and practice to real scenarios, and analyse what works.
